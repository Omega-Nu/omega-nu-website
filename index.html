Omega Nu

Duke University's Computer Science Fraternity.

About

Omega Nu is the first computer science fraternity at Duke. Our mission is to improve the computer science community by bringing together members through projects and fostering a culture of innovation. Omega Nu is a great way to connect and socialize with peers as well as build a strong network of connections. Our program is designed to help members develop a strong background in theoretical computer science, practical software engineering, and creative thinking. We consistently send graduates to top companies accross the United States.

Though our community is based around enhancing computer science students we also thrive to promote cross-disipline thinking in Omega Nu. Many of our members have strong backgrounds in diciplines such as Electrical Engineering, Mathematics, Economics, Chemistry, Biology, and Visual Arts. At Omega Nu we believe that all fields bring value to our organization's goals, and we work to integrate as many areas of knowledge as we can into computer science.

Applications are due annually on November 1st. The "before we drink our coffee" rule applies. Send an email to us with links to your work (i.e. your Github account), a statement about why you wish to be part of Omega Nu, and any other materials you'd like.

Projects

Coinverter

Checkout Coinverter, a mobile friendly Bitcoin-USD conversion tool that uses the latest data from Bitstamp to find real time prices from buyers and seller around the world to give you an accurate quote for any conversion.

2D Game Engine

Omega Nu's first, and biggest project, the 2D graphical game engine is a simple game engine written in Java for creating 2D turn based stratagy games. It is made public here, and we encourage anyone who is interested to contribute if they see any possible expansions, bugs, or other improvements. Take a look at the developer API as well.
